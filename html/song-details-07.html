Older

Sasha Alex Sloan

"Older" by Sasha Alex Sloan, released in 2019, is a heartfelt anthem that resonates with anyone navigating the complexities of growing up. With its poignant lyrics and emotive vocals, the song captures the bittersweet journey of leaving behind youth and facing the challenges of adulthood. Sasha Alex Sloan's raw honesty and vulnerability shine through in every note, creating a powerful and relatable listening experience. "Older" serves as a reminder that while growing older can be daunting, it also brings growth, wisdom, and the strength to face whatever lies ahead.
