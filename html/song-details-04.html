Lucid Dreams

Juice WRLD

"Lucid Dreams" by Juice WRLD, released in 2018, is a soulful track that delves into the aftermath of heartbreak. With its poignant lyrics and mesmerizing melody, the song captures the pain and confusion of lost love. It quickly became a breakout hit, resonating with listeners worldwide and solidifying Juice WRLD's reputation as a rising star in the music industry.
