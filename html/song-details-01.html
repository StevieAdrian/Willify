Blinding Light

The Weeknd

Blinding Light is a song written by The Weeknd back in 2020. This song is about the longing and desperation one feels when separated from a loved one. The Weeknd captures the emotional turmoil and the intense desire to reunite, all set against a backdrop of pulsating synth beats and catchy melodies.
